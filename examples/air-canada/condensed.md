# Air Canada Refund Rules (Plain English)

## By Ticket Type

**Economy Basic**: No refund, no credit, nothing. You're out of luck.

**Other Non-Refundable Tickets**: No cash refund, but you can get credit toward future travel or AC Wallet credit—*only if* you cancel before departure. Subject to change/cancellation fees in your fare rules.

**Refundable Tickets**: Refundable per fare rules.

---

## The 24-Hour Rule

Cancel within 24 hours of purchase → full refund, no penalty.

---

## Involuntary Refunds (Airline's Fault)

Applies when: flight cancelled, delayed, denied boarding, downgraded, or missed connection due to schedule irregularity.

**What you get:**
- Unused portion refunded
- If you're stranded mid-trip and there's no point continuing: full ticket refund + free flight back to origin
- If you completed part of trip and finished on your own: prorated refund based on mileage
- Downgraded to lower class: fare difference refunded
- Paid-for services you didn't receive: refunded

**Timeline**: Airline must refund within 30 days.

---

## General Refunds (Your Choice to Cancel)

**Fully unused ticket**: Fare paid minus cancellation/change fees per fare rules.

**Partially used ticket**: (Fare paid) − (fare for segments actually flown) − cancellation fees. Most restrictive fee applies.

---

## Special Circumstances (Full Refund, No Penalty)

**Jury duty, military duty, or subpoena**: Full refund with documentation.

**Death** (of passenger, immediate family member, or traveling companion):
- Before travel: full refund
- Mid-trip: unused portion refunded, no cancellation fee
- Proof of death required within 90 days

*Immediate family = spouse/partner, children, parents, siblings, in-laws, grandparents, grandchildren, legal guardian, traveling companion*

---

## Time Limits & Fees

- Request refund during ticket validity period
- After 1 year from issue: +$100 over-aged fee (refundable tickets), +$25 (miscellaneous charges)
- Lost paper ticket: +$100 service fee

---

## Who Gets the Refund

Refund goes to original purchaser via original payment method. If bought through travel agent or another airline, refund must go through them.
